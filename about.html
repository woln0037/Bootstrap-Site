About Me

I’m Liz, a Minneapolis-based Engineer and UX Designer.

I am currently a Product Engineer at Target Corporation working on Tabletop products for the Hearth & Hand brand. I have been with Target for about 7 years after being hired on following a successful internship experience with the Target Product Design & Development team. I grew up in Bloomington, MN and went to the University of Minnesota where I studied Industrial & Systems Engineering. While pursuing my engineer degree, I also started taking Product Design classes including a Toy Product Design class. This class lead me to fall in love with the design process and want to pursue a career in Product Design.

Empathy is a big part of who I am and I strive to put people at the center of my design process. I recently completed a UX bootcamp through the University of Minnesota and am really excited about how I can begin to translate all of my existing experience to User Experience design.

When I’m not working, I love to play board games, cook, do crafts, play music, and attend live music and theatre shows.
