About Me

I’m Liz, a Minneapolis-based Product Engineer and UX Designer.

I am a Product Engineer that has designed thousands of products for Target Corporation over a 9+ year career. I grew up in Bloomington, MN and went to the University of Minnesota where I studied Industrial & Systems Engineering. While pursuing my engineer degree, I also started taking Product Design classes including a Toy Product Design class. This class lead me to fall in love with the design process and want to pursue a career in Product Design.

Empathy is a big part of who I am and I strive to put people at the center of my design process. I recently completed a UX/UI bootcamp through the University of Minnesota, as well as a certificate in Product Management from eCornell, and am really excited about how I can leverage what I have learned in my daily work.

When I’m not working, I love to play board games, solve logic/cryptic puzzles, cook, do crafts, play music, and attend live music and theatre shows.
