Hearth & Hand With Magnolia

I led technical development for Kitchen Tabletop products for the Hearth & Hand with Magnolia brand with a focus on continuous improvement and guest satisfaction. My product generated $47.49 million in sales yearly while maintaining an average product rating of 4.5 stars out of 5 across all items. Within the last year, I transitioned to owning development for Decor products.

Teakettle Case Study

The original design felt a little too big compared to what is on the market

I updated the design to be a bit smaller which aligned better with the competition

There were some quality red flags early on with the vendor. To make things more difficult, this development took place during the height of COVID.

Ultimately, the vendor confirmed that the quality would be improved in production and the item went to market. Not long after hitting the shelves, it was recalled for safety concerns.

Fast forward a couple years, I worked on a new and improved teakettle product that hit the stores recently and has been performing very well!
